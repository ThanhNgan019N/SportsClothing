Purchase Policy

At SGDongreeFC, we strive to ensure a seamless and transparent shopping experience for our customers. Please review our purchase policy below to understand the terms and conditions related to buying products from our store.

1. Ordering Process

When placing an order, please ensure:

  • You provide accurate personal and shipping details.
  • Payment is completed successfully before order processing.
  • You review your order before confirming.

2. Payment Methods

We accept the following payment options:

  • Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, AMEX)
  • Bank Transfer
  • Cash on Delivery (Available for select regions)

3. Shipping & Delivery

Our estimated delivery time varies based on location:

  • Domestic Orders: 3 - 7 business days
  • International Orders: 7 - 14 business days

We partner with trusted carriers to ensure timely and secure deliveries.

4. Order Cancellation

You can cancel your order within 24 hours of purchase for a full refund. After this period, the order may already be processed for shipping.

5. Returns & Refunds

We offer a 7-day return policy for defective or incorrect products. Items must be:

  • Unused and in original packaging.
  • Accompanied by a receipt or proof of purchase.

Refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days after the return is approved.
